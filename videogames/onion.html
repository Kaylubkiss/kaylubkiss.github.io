Nowhere to Grow

The second-ever game I made while attending DigiPen. I was excited to work with a team of energetic people, if not a bit intimidated. I leveraged my previous experience with animation and storytelling to pull together the heart of the game. Systems weren't coming easy for me, on the other hand.
